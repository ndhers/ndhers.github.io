McGill Rocket Team

Rocket Team (2018): Member of Aerostructures sub-division, winner of 2018 America Spaceport Cup. Built rocket airframe composite parts (VARI molding): body tube, nose cone, fins, tail. Performed rocket simulations on OpenRocket and involved in CAD to improve aerodynamic performance of nose. CNC machining for mold and surface polishing/sanding of nose cone and body tube.
